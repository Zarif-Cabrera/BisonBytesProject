Mission Statement

Oral health is a silent epidemic with millions in the black community left neglected without adequate access to oral healthcare. Oral health is a window into the overall state of an individual and declining oral health can have devastating consequences to the entire body, leading to horrific consequences inextricably linked to the high rate of chronic diseases in the black community. These include things like strokes, arthritis, and diabetes. In fact, the leading causes of death among black Americans are cancer and heart disease, both of which are connected to poor oral health. Knowing this, it is tragic that oral health is so lacking in availability in disadvantaged communities as well as often unaffordable. We at Bison Smiles could not stand watching this crisis without taking action, and we are committed to developing an equitable solution that has the potential to create a widespread impact. Our ultimate goal is to end the tremendous lack of access to information about oral care in neglected communities, while also making preventative care simple and effective for users to take advantage of. We believe that no one should be deprived of the opportunity to receive treatment and information on oral health, no matter how dire their circumstances may be.
